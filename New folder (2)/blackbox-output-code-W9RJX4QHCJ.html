Nature Facts

Nature provides over 80% of the world's food supply through agriculture and wild resources.

Forests cover about 31% of the Earth's land surface and are home to more than 80% of terrestrial biodiversity.

The Amazon Rainforest produces 20% of the world's oxygen and absorbs vast amounts of CO2.
